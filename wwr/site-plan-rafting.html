Down the River Rafting Site Plan

By Hope Ini Udoka

Overview

Purpose

At Down the River, we aim to provide a refreshing experience on any of our white water rafting trips, while also providing safety and comfort to make your outdoors experience the ultimate one yet. This site plan is a layout that will guide building the website for Down the River's online presence.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on) Our typical audience is people who want a break from the everyday demands of life, People who love the outdoors, want to go on adventures, and be safe while having fun. They are 18-50 years old, and would not mind spending a little extra for safety and comfort.
What do they want? They want to experience nature, and the outdoors and have adventures.
What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They can find information on the trips and services offered, pricing, and safety procedures, and contact us.
