History

Down the River was founded by Sarah and Michael Lawson, a couple with a shared love for nature and a knack for adventure. They started with a single raft and a dream, leading small groups of friends and family on thrilling excursions down the untamed rapids of the Colorado River.

Today, Down the River is a thriving enterprise, but it still retains the intimate, family-owned charm that Sarah and Michael envisioned. Every trip is crafted to create unforgettable memories, with guides who treat guests like family and rivers that challenge and inspire.
